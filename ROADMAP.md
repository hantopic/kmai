# KMAI Development Roadmap

**Document ID:** KMAI-ROAD-001  
**Version:** 0.1.0  
**Last Updated:** July 2026  
**Project:** KMAI (Korean Medicine Artificial Intelligence Platform)

---

# Vision

The KMAI Platform aims to become a modular, multimodal AI platform for Korean Medicine.

Development proceeds in phases, beginning with expert consensus labeling of thyroid ultrasound images and expanding toward multimodal foundation models and precision medicine.

---
